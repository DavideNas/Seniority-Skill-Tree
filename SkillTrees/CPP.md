| Area                            | Dettagli                                          |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Smart Pointers                  | ✅✅✅ (`unique_ptr`, `shared_ptr`, `weak_ptr`)   |
| Lambda & Closures               | ✅✅✅ (cattura, funzioni inline, scope)          |
